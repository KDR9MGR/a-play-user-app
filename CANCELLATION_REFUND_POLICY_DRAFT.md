# A-Play Cancellation & Refund Policy
**Last Updated: April 10, 2026**

---

## Overview

At A-Play, we understand that plans change. This policy outlines our cancellation and refund procedures for event bookings, subscriptions, and other services.

---

## Event Bookings

### Cancellation Timeframes

#### **More than 48 Hours Before Event**
- **Full Refund**: 100% of ticket price returned
- **Processing Time**: 5-7 business days
- **Method**: Refund to original payment method

#### **24-48 Hours Before Event**
- **Partial Refund**: 50% of ticket price returned
- **Processing Time**: 5-7 business days
- **Service Fee**: Non-refundable (payment processing costs)

#### **Less than 24 Hours Before Event**
- **No Refund**: Tickets are non-refundable
- **Exception**: May transfer to another guest (contact support)
- **Special Circumstances**: Medical emergencies considered on case-by-case basis

#### **After Event Starts**
- **No Refund**: Tickets are non-refundable after event start time

### How to Cancel Event Bookings

1. **In-App Cancellation**
   - Go to "My Bookings" in the app
   - Select the booking you wish to cancel
   - Tap "Cancel Booking"
   - Review cancellation terms
   - Submit cancellation request

2. **Email Support**
   - Email: support@aplayapp.com
   - Subject: "Cancellation Request - [Booking ID]"
   - Include: Booking reference number and reason for cancellation

3. **Phone Support** (Business Hours)
   - Phone: +233 [Your Support Number]
   - Hours: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM GMT

### Special Event Cancellations

**Sold-Out Events**: Non-refundable 72 hours before event (high demand)

**VIP/Premium Tickets**: Subject to stricter cancellation terms (outlined at purchase)

**Multi-Day Events**: Each day considered separately for cancellation purposes

## Subscription Cancellations

### Monthly Subscriptions
- **Cancel Anytime**: No cancellation fee
- **Access Continues**: Until end of current billing period
- **No Partial Refunds**: Payment for current month is non-refundable
- **How to Cancel**:
  - In-App: Profile → Subscription → Manage → Cancel
  - iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → A-Play → Cancel
  - Android: Google Play → Subscriptions → A-Play → Cancel

### Free Trial Period
- **Cancel Before Trial Ends**: No charge
- **Grace Period**: 24 hours before trial end date
- **Automatic Renewal**: Unless cancelled before trial expires

### Subscription Refunds
- **First 3 Days**: Full refund if unsatisfied (one-time courtesy)
- **After 3 Days**: No refunds for unused portion of subscription
- **Exception**: Technical issues preventing service access

## Restaurant & Club Bookings (Coming Soon)

### Table Reservations
- **More than 6 Hours Notice**: Full refund
- **3-6 Hours Notice**: 50% refund
- **Less than 3 Hours**: No refund

### Pre-Paid Food & Beverage Orders
- **More than 12 Hours Notice**: Full refund
- **Less than 12 Hours**: No refund (perishable items)

## Event Organizer Cancellations

### If Event is Cancelled by Organizer
- **Full Refund**: 100% of ticket price + service fees
- **Processing Time**: 3-5 business days
- **Notification**: Email and push notification
- **Alternative**: Credit for future events (if preferred)

### Event Postponement
- **Tickets Remain Valid**: For rescheduled date
- **Cannot Attend New Date**: Full refund available
- **Request Period**: 14 days from postponement announcement

### Event Changes
- **Minor Changes**: No refund (venue, time adjustments)
- **Major Changes**: Refund available (headliner cancellation, significant format change)

## Exceptions & Special Circumstances

### Medical Emergencies
- **Considered Case-by-Case**: Contact support with documentation
- **Required**: Doctor's note or hospital documentation
- **Decision**: Within 3 business days

### Force Majeure
- **Natural Disasters**: Full refund if event cancelled
- **Government Restrictions**: Full refund if attendance prohibited
- **Pandemic/Health Crisis**: Per government guidelines

### Technical Issues
- **App Malfunction**: Full refund if unable to access tickets
- **Payment Errors**: Immediate refund + investigation
- **Contact**: support@aplayapp.com immediately

## Refund Request Process

### Step-by-Step

1. **Submit Request**
   - Via app or email support@aplayapp.com
   - Provide booking reference number
   - State reason for cancellation

2. **Review**
   - Request reviewed within 24-48 hours
   - Email confirmation sent

3. **Approval**
   - Approved refunds processed automatically
   - Denied requests include explanation + appeal option

4. **Processing**
   - Refund initiated to original payment method
   - Processing time varies by method (see table above)

5. **Confirmation**
   - Email confirmation when refund completed
   - Track status in app: My Bookings → [Booking] → Refund Status

## Non-Refundable Items

The following are **non-refundable** under any circumstances:

- Service and processing fees (unless event cancelled by organizer)
- Tickets purchased through third-party resale
- Tickets marked as "Final Sale" at purchase
- Gift cards and vouchers (unless required by law)
- Past events or expired bookings
